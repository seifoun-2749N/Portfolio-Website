About Me

Passionate About Visual Communication

I'm Seif Eddine Chatti, a 17-year-old student at LPA from Tunisia, a passionate and experienced graphic designer with over five years in the field.

I began my journey early, constantly striving to improve my skills and creativity. My dedication led him to spend two years as part of the Red Crescent media team, where I gained valuable experience in humanitarian communication and design. Since June 2025, I have been serving as the Graphic Design Manager for CRLPBT (Red Crescent), overseeing impactful visual projects.

Alongside this, I worked as Media Manager for Flex VTC, a gaming community, from April to September 2025, where I specialized in creating engaging visuals and community-focused media. Over the years, I have participated in numerous media events, designed banners for YouTube videos, and built a strong foundation in both branding and digital content creation.

Always seeking growth, Seif Eddine continues to push himself to learn, adapt, and evolve as a designer.
